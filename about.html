BEN MARTIN

Director // Producer

Raised in Atlanta by two architect parents, Ben grew up in the vibrant Little Five Points neighborhood, surrounded by music, art, and creativity. After working across nearly every department in film production, Ben realized what he truly loves is guiding a project from script to screen—being involved in every creative step along the way.

We take our work seriously, but not ourselves. The goal is always to create something showstopping—while keeping it fun, collaborative, and human.

Ben’s passion lies in building great crews, taking care of people, and making visually stunning, emotionally resonant work. With a strong background in Cinematography and Visual Effects, he brings both a technical eye and a storyteller’s heart to every project.

john roche

Director // Producer

John has been making films since he was old enough to hold the not-so-compact camcorders of yesteryear. Growing up in the Atlanta suburbs he would entertain himself and the other neighborhood kids making skits and sketches. He graduated from UGA with a degree in Marketing to appease the old gods (his parents) and began working at an in town creative agency out of college. This would prove to be unfulfilling, so he founded Juice Factory with his friend Ben.

We joke that I’m the Juice and Ben is the factory. I think that’s in reference to always trying to push the envelope, make it weirder, write the risqué jokes.

As a writer-director, John’s work spans national ad campaigns, music videos, short films, and a published collection of short stories and poetry. Whether wrangling a wild idea into a tight :30 or building a world from scratch, he brings humor, style, and the right kind of chaos to make the magic happen.

meaghan cafferty

Brand Strategist

Raised in New Jersey and now based in Atlanta, Meagh is a powerhouse of design and strategy. With experience across both agency and in-house roles, her work spans print, merch, web, and motion.

I’m here to make sure people feel something—and want to stick around. I connect story, strategy, and design to build brands that actually mean something to the people who love them.

Meagh is the glue between brand and visuals—ensuring every project tells a cohesive story. Equal parts design wizard and project management pro, Meagh brings energy, clarity, and magic to everything we do. We’re lucky to have her.
